# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients evaluating whether to hire Elyxia: private game server owners (RF Online, Ran Online communities), small business owners, and e-commerce operators. They arrive from Facebook groups, Discord servers, or referrals, usually on mobile or a mid-range desktop, and decide within a minute whether this developer looks credible enough to message.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio that positions Elyxia as a senior front-end developer for gaming platforms, MMORPG server websites, e-commerce storefronts, and modern web applications. Success is a client clicking a live project link and then opening the contact section.

## Brand Personality

Precise, quietly confident, production-focused. The site itself is the proof of skill: restrained editorial dark design (zinc-950, white as the only accent, Inter), real shipped work with real URLs, no decoration that a client could mistake for template filler.

## Anti-references

- Glassmorphism, neon/cyberpunk gaming tropes, code-rain, gradient text
- Generic floating card grids and stock developer illustrations
- Template-shaped AI copy (em dashes, buzzword strings)
- Anything that reads "gaming website" rather than "professional who builds gaming websites"

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the portfolio piece.** Every detail (spacing, motion, focus states) is itself evidence of seniority.
2. **Real work first.** Live URLs, real screenshots, honest results copy. Never ship placeholder content to production.
3. **White is the only accent.** Hierarchy comes from type scale, weight, and zinc steps, never from color.
4. **Newest and strongest work leads.** Ordering is a sales decision, not a chronology.
5. **Brand only.** Elyxia, never a real name.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all meaningful text against zinc-950. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Keyboard-visible focus states on every interactive element.
